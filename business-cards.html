Choosing the Right Finish

Glossy cards suit bold colors, photography and brands that want to stand out in a stack. Matte cards feel more premium and are easier to write on — popular with consultants, real estate agents and law firms. Spot UV adds a raised, glossy layer over specific elements (a logo, a name) against a matte background, giving a tactile, high-end finish favored by hospitality and luxury retail brands in Dubai.

File Specifications

To avoid delays, print-ready artwork should be supplied as a PDF in CMYK color mode at 300 DPI, with a 3mm bleed on all sides and text kept at least 4mm from the trim edge. Don't have a designer? Our custom design service starts at AED 99 and includes print-ready files.
