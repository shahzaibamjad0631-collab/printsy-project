Design for Print vs. Design for Digital

Print design (business cards, flyers, banners) needs to be built in CMYK color at print resolution with bleed and trim marks — different requirements from a social media post, which is built in RGB at screen resolution. Our team designs correctly for whichever the piece needs, so what you approve is exactly what prints or posts.

When to Start With Design vs. Bring Your Own

If you already have a logo and brand guidelines, we can lay out your print materials to match them exactly. If you're starting from scratch, our Brand Identity package (AED 799) builds the whole foundation — logo, guidelines, business cards and social templates — in one go.
