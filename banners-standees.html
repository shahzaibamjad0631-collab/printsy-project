Choosing Roll-Up vs. X-Banner

Roll-up banners retract into a weighted base — the most common choice for exhibitions and reception areas because they look polished and set up fast. X-banners use a lightweight X-shaped frame and are typically more affordable, making them a good fit for temporary retail promotions or multi-location campaigns.

Artwork Guidelines

Large-format artwork should be supplied at a reduced scale (e.g. 1:10) at 150 DPI minimum in CMYK, with key text and logos kept away from the edges. We can also handle the design in-house through our custom design service.
